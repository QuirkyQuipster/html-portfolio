Best movies according to Komal

My top 3 anime-movies of all-time

Weathering with you

It's one of my favourite movie.The genre of the movie is thriller,fantasy and disaster.The animation is really good,nice plot,some fun parts too.Theme of the movie is weather how t affects the people in complex way. It tells us what it means to live in an age of climate change and shoulder some of the responsibility for altering our world.

Ride your wave

It's a romance fantasy movie.The art wasn't that great but the storyline is pretty good.Grab your tissues as it'll surely make you cry while watching.

Spirited away

The best movie in studio Ghibli. You will definitely love it if you are an anime fan.It's genre is fantasy,adventure and mystery.The expirience of watching this movie is extraordinary;it was magical ,weird and dreamlike with exploring each mysterious characters having different abilities.
